Online Payment Fraud Detector

The transaction evaluation application employs K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm to detect potential fraud risks of online payments based on a dataset containing details of normal and fraud transaction. With the entered details of any individual transaction by users, the application predicts its likelihood of being fraud with a binary outcome. The application helps its user individuals and organisations to filter and spot highly suspicious frauds from a large amount of transactions in a more efficient way.

EU Employee Wage Estimator

The EU Employee Wage Estimator is developed to assist member states' social welfare institutes and government agencies to predict an individual's employee wage and determine whether an individual, who is either unemployed or employed, will be or is underpaid or not. After understanding the approximate employee wage of an individual, the government and other institutes can act to help one with living cost pressures by offering various forms of support. Eventually, the estimator helps to reduce social inequality in the aspects of race, gender, health care and education level in the labour market and governance in general in the European Union.
